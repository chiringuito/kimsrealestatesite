Your Home Buying Journey

Choosing to buy a home is one of the biggest decisions you'll make in your life. It's a complex process that requires careful planning and the help of a professional. Kim Reidy has guided hundreds of buyers through the Seattle market and is here to make the process smooth, informed, and exciting.



1

Get Pre-Approved

Before you start shopping, meet with a lender to get pre-approved for a mortgage. This tells you exactly how much home you can afford and makes your offer more competitive.

2

Define Your Needs

Make a list of must-haves and nice-to-haves. Consider neighborhood, school districts, commute, size, and style. Kim is known as a "neighborhood whisperer" and will help you find the perfect area.

3

Start Your Search

Work with Kim to tour homes that meet your criteria. Use the property search tool to browse by price range, neighborhood, beds, and baths — updated in real time.

4

Make an Offer

When you find the right home, Kim will craft a competitive offer based on market analysis, current comps, and negotiation strategy. In Seattle's competitive market, strategy matters.

5

Due Diligence & Inspection

Once under contract, you'll schedule inspections and review disclosures. Kim will guide you through findings and negotiate repairs or credits on your behalf.

6

Close & Move In

Final walkthrough, sign documents, get your keys! Kim stays with you through every step of closing to ensure a smooth, stress-free finish.
