Seattle's Neighborhoods

Seattle is a city of distinct, vibrant neighborhoods — each with its own personality, pace, and community. As a Senior Broker and Director of Relocation with deep roots in this city, Kim Reidy knows these neighborhoods intimately. Whether you're drawn to waterfront living, walkable urban energy, or quiet tree-lined streets, explore the areas below to find the perfect fit for your lifestyle.

Ballard

A popular waterfront neighborhood north of downtown with a rich Scandinavian history, trendy restaurants, craft breweries, and a beloved farmers market.

Beacon Hill

Capitol Hill

Denny Triangle

An urban triangle seamlessly integrating professional and residential life, with restaurants, lounges, cafes, and the energy of Seattle's tech corridor.

Downtown Seattle

Perfect for those who love the energy of big-city living — world-class dining, the waterfront, Pike Place Market, and unbeatable walkability all at your door.

First Hill
